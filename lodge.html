Verification Tasks

Skill Points are earned by applying knowledge, not performing labor. They represent verified competence and discipline, unlocking deeper guidance and curated opportunities.

Stewardship Action: Weekly Expense Audit

Submit your categorized 7-day expense log from Academy Module 2 for verification and feedback.

  • Proof: Clear screenshot or document link
  • Standard: Categories + totals + one reflection
  • Reward: 25 Skill Points + Written Feedback

Verification Project: Personal Risk Rule Analysis

Expand on your “Risk Rules” from Module 4. Analyze a common financial scam and write a 300-word guide on how to identify it.

  • Proof: Document link (readable + structured)
  • Standard: 3 warning signs + 3 protections
  • Reward: 50 Skill Points + Qualification for Level 2 Consultation

Submission Protocol

  • Use your verified name and email (same identity used for approval).
  • Provide proof clearly (screenshots must show dates when possible).
  • One submission at a time. Quality over speed.
  • Verification outcomes: Approved (points awarded) / Needs revision (feedback provided).
