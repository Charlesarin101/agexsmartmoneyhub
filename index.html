Task Creator Guide (Copy-Paste Ready)

AGEx is trust-first. To post a task, you must write it like a contract: clear deliverables, proof required, and a realistic reward.

  • 1) Title: Clear and specific (what will be delivered?)
  • 2) Description: Step-by-step requirements (avoid vague instructions)
  • 3) Deliverables: What counts as ‚Äúdone‚Äù?
  • 4) Proof: Screenshot / links / document / report
  • 5) Reward: Choose within the system guidance range
  • 6) Deadline: Realistic completion time

In Phase 1, tasks are small and learning-aligned. In Phase 2, verified creators unlock larger tasks.

Example (Allowed)

Title: ‚ÄúWrite 10 caption options for AGEx Smart Money Hub‚Äù

  • Deliverable: 10 captions + 10 hashtag sets
  • Proof: Paste in Google Doc link
  • Reward: $0.05 (Level 2 task)
  • Deadline: 24 hours

Example (Not Allowed)

‚ÄúGet me 100 followers for my Facebook page‚Äù

  • Reason: encourages inauthentic engagement; risks penalties; damages credibility.
  • Allowed alternative: ‚ÄúCreate 7-day content plan + outreach invites + engagement report.‚Äù
