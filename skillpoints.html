What It Is

Skill Points (SP) are a learning and verification ledger. They prove you learned, practiced, and produced evidence — not just claims.

How You Earn

  • Complete a module lesson
  • Pass a short quiz
  • Submit a verified mini-project
  • Attend a practice session
  • Maintain learning consistency

The Bridge to Value

Pathway A — Direct Unlocks

SP unlocks premium resources: advanced modules, templates, live mentoring sessions, and review support.

Pathway B — Partnership Opportunities

SP becomes a proof record you can show to trusted partners for interview priority, internships, and verified opportunities.

Future Vision

In future updates, Skill Points may convert into tokens or withdrawal value when the ecosystem reaches full verification scale.
